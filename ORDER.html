Terms & Conditions For Orders


  • We accept orders from Tamilnadu, Pondicherry, Andhra, Telangana, Karnataka, and Kerala.

  • Customers of other states apart from Tamil Nadu and Pondicherry are asked to place their order atleast 1 month .

  • Products will be dispatched only after your entire payment is confirmed

  • Delivery is done by third party agencies

  • Customers of other states can receive their parcels within 14 days from the time of their bill settlement.

  • Customers can also suggest their favourable transport agency for delivery, If they have.

  • Goods will be packed only in carboard boxes, following all precautionary safety measures

  • We do not take responsibility for any damages incurred or any delay made by transport

  • Thank you for choosing and supporting us

Steps To Place Order Enquiry And Payment Method


You can place your order enquiry by filling the below form. You must give your full address and contact details correctly. Next You can place your order enquiry by clicking "Submit" button. On clicking "Submit", your order enquiry will be placed. As soon as we receive your order enquiry , we will contact you within 24 hours to clarify your queries and tell you payment method, via mobile number that you have given us earlier.
